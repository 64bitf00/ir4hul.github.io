Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to your quick questions.

What is the schedule of the tournament?

Game-A-Thon 2018 starts off on 12th October 2018 as event under graVITas'18, the annual tech fest of VIT University, Vellore. The tournament continues for 72 hours, i.e 12th, 13th and 14th October 2018.

Start time : 12/10/2018 8:00:00 PM
End time : 14/10/2018 7:00:00 AM

How do i participate in the tournament?

You need to register for the event using the graVITas'18 website. Visit the website here or use the register button present in the menu.

Sign up and login on the graVITas'18 website and find the event named Game-A-Thon '18 under the non-technical section.

Where do i choose which game to play?

We will be providing a form to everyone with the option to choose the game you will be playing and also specify the details of your team in case of team games. Keep checking this website for all the updates.

Also follow us on social media platforms for quick updates.

I am not a student of VIT. How do I register?

Register using the Non-VITians option while signing up on the graVITas'18 website. Alternatively use this link.

I don't have a team, what do I do?

We will provide you with a form where you can provide your game experience details. We will match you with a team accordingly.
